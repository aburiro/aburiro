About

Bakers
Passionately baking since 1880, Schulstad Bakery Solutions is a brand that stands proudly upon its heritage and expertise, while embracing the most modern baking techniques. Our dedicated bakers combine over a century of baking experience and knowledge of tradition to develop delicious pastries with superior taste, aroma and structure. Schulstad offers a flavorful range of gourmet Danish pastries and croissants that are ready-to-bake and easy to customize for quick, casual and convenient services. From icing and glazes, to unique toppings and decorations, to innovative baking ideas, there are endless possibilities with Schulstad pastries.
